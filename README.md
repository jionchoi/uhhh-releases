  # uhhh.ai 0.1.0

  The first public release of **uhhh.ai** — the fastest way to find the word that's on the tip of
  your tongue, in any app you're typing in.

  ## What it does
  Stuck on a word? Press the hotkey, describe what you mean in plain language, and uhhh.ai gives
  you the word — then drops it right back where your cursor was.

  - **Works everywhere you type** — Gmail, Docs, Slack, your editor, anywhere.
  - **One hotkey:** `⌘ + ;` on macOS, `Ctrl + ;` on Windows.
  - **Speak it instead:** hold `Ctrl + '` to describe the word out loud (English).
  - **Describe it your way** — type a loose, plain-language description; you don't need to know
  the word, that's the point.

  ## Download
  - **macOS (Apple Silicon — M1–M4):** `Uhhh.ai_0.1.0_aarch64.dmg`
  - **macOS (Intel):** `Uhhh.ai_0.1.0_x64.dmg`
  - **Windows 10/11:** `Uhhh.ai_0.1.0_x64-setup.exe`

  > Not sure which Mac you have?  → Apple menu → About This Mac. "Apple M-series" = Apple Silicon.

  ## First launch
  - **macOS** asks for **Accessibility** (so the hotkey works while another app is focused) and,
  for voice, **Microphone**. Grant both in System Settings → Privacy & Security, then relaunch.
  - **Windows** may show **“Windows protected your PC.”** That's expected for a brand-new app —
  it's safely signed (publisher: *Jion Choi*). Click **More info → Run anyway**.
